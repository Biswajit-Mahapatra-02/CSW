Im•pu•ta•tion

  1. The act of imputing
  2. Whatever is ascribed or charged; especially censured or reproached

Use:

  • I resent the imputation that I'm nice to Grandmother because she has money.
  • It turned out that the child's imputation that his brother had hidden his toys was incorrect when the toy was found in the child's bedroom
