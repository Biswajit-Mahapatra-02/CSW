Cal•is•then•ics

  1. Light gymnastics to promote grace and health: construed as plural
  2. The science of such exercises construed as singular

Use:

  • three women swung Indian clubs while performing callisthenics in unison
  • Weight bearing exercises and strength training, such as lifting weights or callisthenics, build muscles.
